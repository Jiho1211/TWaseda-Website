Entrance Guidelines

Home > Entrance Guidleines

Regular recruitment

Tokyo Waseda Foreign Langugae School recruits new stduents four times a yera (in January, April, July and October).
The process from filling in the application to final admission is given below.

Submit entrance applications and pay entrance fee Hand in Visa Application documents to Tokyo Immigration Bureau from Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School Certificate of Eligiblity from Tokyo Immigration Bureau After paying school expenses, get the Certificate of Eligibility at Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School.

The Application Process

1

Submit Application Documents and Entrance and Screening Fee

2

Send of Application Documents to Tokyo Immigration Bureau from School

3

Issue of Resident Certificate by Tokyo Immigration Bureau

4

Receipt of Resident Certificate from School after Payment of Tuition

5

Visa Application to Local Japenese Embassy (Consulate)

6

Visa Acquisition

7

Entry to Japan

8

School Entrance

Application Deadlines

  • Application will close when full capacity is reached.
  • Please submit the document for the visa as soon as possible because it takes time to investigate the documents and additional documents may be required.
  • Feel free to contact the school office as the deadlines could change

Short-term Applicants

  • We also accept applications from those with Short-term visa, Dependent visa, Working Holiday visa
  • The class is determined after taking a simple test.
  • Applications may vary depending on the situation so feel free to contact the school office for further information.

Financial Aid

  • Japanese Government (MEXT) Scholarship (360,000yen per year)
  • Internal (School) Scholarship (120,000yen per year)
  • One-time awards for outstanding performance, highest grades, and attendance rates, Shinyukai award, etc.
