University Admission Support

This is a unique instruction program created by Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School. It supports students who plan to enter top universities in Japan and helps to improve their academic performance. For those entering well-known undergraduate and graduate schools in Tokyo, Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School offers an advanced EJU Preparation Course which includes not only Japanese, but also a more integrated range of subjects such as politics, economics, history, and culture. Moreover, we recommend and provide various courses according to students' goals, such as English Test Preparation, Math, Business Japanese Conversation, Mini-Thesis Plans, Kanji Lectures, Grammar Lectures, and special courses for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test.

EJU Preparation Course

It supports students who want to enter top universities in Japan and improve their academic performance. Our EJU Preparation Course helps students enter well-known undergraduate and graduate schools in Tokyo by not only equipping students with Japanese language skills, but also with a range of subjects such as politics, economics, history, and culture.

EJU Math Preparation Course

Students who take the EJU are free to take either Math for Liberal Arts or Math for Natural Sciences.

JLPT Preparation Course

This preparation course helps students to enhance their listening, reading, and grammar skills and achieve high scores for entering Japanese national universities.

Graduate School Admissions Seminars

These seminars are for students planning to apply for graduate school. Our qualified faculty members who graduated from prestigious universities and graduate schools provide various seminars regarding admission and examination processes.
