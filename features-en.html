Features

Eastablish on Christian Values

Our school is established on Christian values, and we strive to help our students' academics and life in Japan.

Main Campus

In our 9-story building, we provide optimum conditions and equipment for effective learning. Our school has multi-national staff who are proficient in various languages, classrooms with capacity over 50 people, and electronic blackboards to facilitate students' learning. The school is located within a 3-minute walk from Seibu Shinjuku Station.

Shin-Okubo Campus

Our school is a 5-story building, located within a minute walk from Shin-Okubo Station, and provides optimum conditions for effective learning with up-to-date facilities, We also provide Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) classes and Examination for Japanese University (EJU) classes, We provide students with opportunities to experience Japanese culture through extracurricular activities, Students can also see the fruits of their studying through speech contests.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Japanese Government (MEXT) Scholarship (360,000 yen per year) / Internal (School) Scholarship (120,000 yen per year) / One-time awards for outstanding performance, highest grades, and attendance rates, Shinyukai award, etc.

EJU Preparation Course/ JLPT Preparation Course/ Graduate School Admissions Seminars

Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School exclusively offers a special program to help students prepare for top-ranked universities.

Special Classes for Individual Goals

We offer various classes such as English Conversation. Business Japanese Conversation, Mini Thesis Projects, Kanji Lectures, Grammar Lectures Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) Preparation Course, Mathematics, News, and EJU Preparation course.

Personalized Academic Counseling

Along with providing thorough individual academic guidance, we also help students practice oral interviews. write essays and research proposals, and fill in or submit admission documents.

Special Admission by Recommendation

Students can enter one of our affiliated universities without entrance examinations if recommended by Tokyo Waseda Foreign Language School.

Various Annual Events

Through various extracurricular activities, students can experience Japanese culture directly and learn practical Japanese.

Academic and Everyday Life Assistance By Staff Speaking 6 Languages

Fluent in Chinese, Korean, Vietnamese, Burmese, Nepalese, and Sinhala-our experienced specialists are always there for you.
